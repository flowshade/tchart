T Chart Generator

By SameeraMurthy


Easily plan and organize for essay portions on tests.
Put your most important ideas on the left,
and support those ideas with specific examples on the right.
(Created for CCFanClub 2nd jam)



How to delete an item

Hover over the item, and ctrl + click to delete.



How to add an item

Click on the menu and click +.



How to save your T-chart

Click on the menu, click the print icon,
and a modal should pop up.
Click print or set the printer
option to save as pdf, and save!
